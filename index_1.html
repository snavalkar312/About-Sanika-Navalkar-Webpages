Forensics


Forensic competition is a contest between individuals or teams in various argument and advocacy skills. Individual events in speech include public speaking, limited preparation, and acting and interpretation and are a part of forensics competitions. I personally do expository which is where you craft an original speech,and describe, clarify,illustrate, or define an object, idea, concept, or process.
