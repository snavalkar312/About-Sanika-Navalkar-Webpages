HOSA


Known as Health Occupations Students of America, HOSA is an international career and technical student organization endorsed by the U.S. Department of Education and the Health Science Technology Education Division of ACTE. At our school it is a club for kids interested in the health sciences, like me! I joined HOSA because I like to take part in clubs that can hep me gain knowledge and make new friends. HOSA relates to me because I want to follow a career path in health science and HOSA seems like the perfect extracurricular activity to help expand my interest in that.
